### Colors:
- **Primary Gradient**: #FF5A87 → #8A2BE2
- **BFF Gradient**: Teal → Blue
- **Success**: #4CAF50 (Green)
- **White**: #FFFFFF with alpha variants
- **Shadows**: Accent colors @ 0.2-0.4 alpha

- **XLarge**: 32w, 32h
